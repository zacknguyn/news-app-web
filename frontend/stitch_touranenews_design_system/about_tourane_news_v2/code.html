A working newsroom

We believe that an independent press is the cornerstone of a functioning society. Tourane News was established not as a platform for opinion, but as an engine for verifiable facts. In an era where information is often diluted by algorithmic bias and click-driven narratives, our newsroom stands as a bulwark for structural integrity and high-density reporting.

Our methodology is rooted in the Minimal Swiss movement—prioritizing clarity, editorial authority, and restraint. We reject modern digital clutter in favor of a "flat paper" philosophy, where hierarchy is established through precise typography and rigorous fact-checking, rather than superficial decoration.

The journalists at Tourane News operate under a strict mandate: observe, verify, and report. We utilize a fixed-fluid hybrid grid inspired by traditional newspaper layouts to deliver a dense, sober, and uncompromised view of global events. Our loyalty is entirely to the truth, unburdened by corporate influence or partisan allegiance.

Timeline

  • 2018

    Inception

    Founded in Geneva as a response to the erosion of localized investigative journalism.

  • 2020

    The Swiss-Grid Shift

    Adoption of our current minimalist editorial framework, standardizing high-density data reporting.

  • 2022

    Global Expansion

    Opened investigative bureaus in London, Tokyo, and New York.

  • 2024

    Independent Network

    Transitioned to a fully independent cooperative model owned by the newsroom staff.

Editorial Principles

  3. Transparent sourcing.
