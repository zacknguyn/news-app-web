HOW SCORES ARE CALCULATED expand_more

The Tourane News trust algorithm operates on a zero-sum, continuous evaluation model. All new accounts begin with a baseline score of 500. Points are accrued through sustained, high-quality engagement, including accurate tip submissions, constructive community moderation, and consistent adherence to our editorial mandate over time.

Deductions are applied automatically when interactions breach our civility standards or when submitted information is verified as false by our editorial desk. Severe violations may result in immediate score suppression or account restriction.
