How it works

We operate on a transparent ledger. Every article is subjected to rigorous fact-checking protocols by an elected committee of subscribers before publication. Our codebase and financial records are fully public, ensuring absolute accountability.

The news is curated not by algorithms designed to maximize engagement, but by editorial consensus focused strictly on factual density and societal relevance.
